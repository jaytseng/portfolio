about

Born in Taipei, Taiwan and initially studying engineering, I am an amalgamation of multiple disciplines and cultures. Now as a student of SIAT in Canada, I strive to integrate excellent visual design elements into my technical projects, while bringing the technical knowledge into my design projects. My passion is enabling human and technological interactions through design as well as empowering people through storytelling and audio visual experiences.
