about the project

In making our first short film, all members agreed that we cared about the environment and wanted to bring awareness to environmental issues in a comical and entertaining way. We decided to tackle waterway conservation and preservation because littering and pollution are current problems, especially on trails and nearby waterways. We also wanted to utilize B.C.’s beautiful forests as a background for the aesthetics of our film. Our goal is to encourage people to respect nature and recycle plastic bottles. Better yet, we hope people will opt to use reusable water bottles instead of plastic bottles. Ultimately, Stream Productions wants to leave our viewers with this message.
