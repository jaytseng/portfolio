//about: Who I am & what I've done.

Designed in Taiwan, Made in Canada.

Cross disciplines, mediums, & perspectives.

Born in Taiwan and initially studying mechatronics system engineering, I am an amalgamation of multiple disciplines and cultures. Now as Canadian BSc graduate of design & media at School of Interactive Arts & Technology, I strive to integrate user centric design elements into my technical projects, while bringing the technical knowledge into my design projects. I enjoy being challenged with wicked problems and my passion is enabling human and technological interactions through design as well as empowering people through storytelling and audio-visual experiences.
